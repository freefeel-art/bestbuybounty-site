Component 1: Tre-en-en Grain Concentrates

Introduced in 1958 — the world's first phytonutrient supplement. Cold-pressed concentrates from wheat germ, rice bran, and soybeans deliver the lipid and sterol fraction that modern grain refining removes. Cell membranes require these lipids and sterols to maintain the fluidity and permeability that enables efficient nutrient transport, cellular signalling, and metabolic waste export.

Texas A&M University research (1987) documented improved nutrient utilisation efficiency in subjects receiving the Tre-en-en lipid fraction. The term "phytonutrient" did not enter mainstream science until the 1990s — Tre-en-en predated the concept by 32 years.

Component 2: Carotenoid Complex

A broad-spectrum carotenoid supplement from 8 whole-food sources: tomato oleoresin (lycopene 1,200 mcg), marigold extract (lutein/zeaxanthin 410 mcg), carrot oleoresin (beta-carotene, alpha-carotene), red bell pepper, spinach, strawberry, apricot, and peach. Delivered in an olive oil base for optimal absorption.

USDA-affiliated research documented a 37% increase in immune capacity over 20 days of supplementation, with enhanced natural killer cell activity and lymphocyte proliferation. The mixed carotenoid formula produced greater immune benefits than beta-carotene supplementation alone. Published in the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition (PMID: 8147336).

Component 3: Omega 3 Plus

All eight omega-3 fatty acids from ultra-purified fish oil in natural triglyceride form — the same structural form as in whole fish. Research shows triglyceride form absorbs up to 70% more effectively than ethyl ester form (the form used in most mass-market fish oil supplements). Molecularly distilled and third-party tested for heavy metals, PCBs, and dioxins every batch.

NeoLife SAB member Arianna Carughi, Ph.D., has presented research on omega-3 supplementation and membrane fluidity indicators — connecting directly to the cellular nutrition philosophy: cell membrane fatty acid composition affects nutrient transport efficiency.

Component 4: Essential Vitamins

A phytoenzyme-enhanced vitamin and mineral complex providing essential micronutrients alongside natural food cofactors that support absorption and utilisation. The phytoenzyme enhancement reflects the SAB's Potency standard: vitamins delivered with the co-factors present in whole food perform differently than isolated synthetic vitamins delivered without them.

The System Rationale: Why All Four Together

The cellular nutrition philosophy underlying Pro Vitality+ holds that cell membrane function is the foundational layer. Tre-en-en prepares the membrane environment — without adequate lipids and sterols, the transport proteins embedded in cell membranes operate at reduced efficiency. Carotenoid Complex and Omega 3 Plus provide phytonutrient and fatty acid support. Essential Vitamins complete the micronutrient layer. All four components depend on functioning cellular transport for their effectiveness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Pro Vitality+ and a standard multivitamin?

A standard multivitamin delivers isolated vitamins and minerals. Pro Vitality+ adds three categories that no multivitamin addresses: grain-derived lipids and sterols (Tre-en-en) for cell membrane function, a broad-spectrum carotenoid complex from eight whole-food sources, and all eight omega-3 fatty acids from ultra-purified fish oil in natural triglyceride form.

Can I take the components separately?

Yes. Each of the four components is available as a standalone product: Tre-en-en (item #3130, 120 capsules), Carotenoid Complex (item #3300, 90 softgels), Omega 3 Plus (standalone), and Essential Vitamins. Pro Vitality+ delivers all four in convenient daily packets.

What did the USDA research on Carotenoid Complex show?

USDA-affiliated researchers documented a 37% increase in immune capacity over 20 days of Carotenoid Complex supplementation, including enhanced natural killer cell activity and lymphocyte proliferation. The mixed carotenoid formula produced greater immune benefits than beta-carotene supplementation alone. Published in the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition (PMID: 8147336).

Why is natural triglyceride omega-3 form better?

Natural triglyceride is how omega-3 fatty acids exist in whole fish. Ethyl ester is a cheaper industrial processing product. Research shows natural triglyceride absorbs up to 70% more effectively than ethyl ester, particularly without dietary fat present. Most mass-market fish oil supplements use the ethyl ester form.

How long does it take to see results from Pro Vitality+?

The USDA immune function research measured outcomes over 20-day supplementation periods. Cell membrane lipid composition changes occur over comparable or longer timeframes. Pro Vitality+ is a foundational system designed for consistent daily use — not acute supplementation. Individual results vary.
