Why We Don't Publish Standard Testimonials

We apply a different standard on this site: the same factual, source-cited approach used throughout.

What this means in practice: we will not publish "this product changed my life" testimonials without specific, documented detail. Individual Variation: What to Expect

Clinical averages describe population-level outcomes, not individual guarantees. The 37% immune capacity increase from Carotenoid Complex research is a mean across study participants — individual responses varied. The 38% wrinkle reduction from Nutriance Rejuvenating Rich Cream is an upper bound in clinical testing — "up to" means the maximum documented, not the universal outcome.

This is not a limitation specific to NeoLife — it is how all clinical nutrition research works. The realistic expectation is that consistent use of products with documented clinical evidence is more likely to produce meaningful outcomes than products without it. The SAB's research record is the relevant differentiator — not anecdotal testimonials from either direction.
