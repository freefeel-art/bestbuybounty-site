What Is GR2 Glycemic Response Control Technology?

GR2 Technology is NeoLife's framework for structuring food intake to manage the body's glycemic and insulinemic response. High-glycemic eating patterns produce rapid blood glucose spikes followed by insulin surges, which promote fat storage and suppress fat oxidation. Low-glycemic approaches moderate this response, creating a metabolic environment more conducive to using stored fat as energy.

The GR2 protocol operationalizes this by structuring meals around protein (NeoLifeShake as the primary vehicle), strategic timing, and the displacement of high-glycemic foods. The Mediterranean diet framework provides the broader dietary context: high in vegetables, legumes, whole grains, olive oil, and fish; low in processed carbohydrates and refined sugar.

Practical Considerations

The program requires dietary structure, not just a shake: GR2 Technology is a meal-composition framework. Replacing one meal with a shake while continuing high-glycemic eating patterns at other meals will not produce the metabolic environment the GR2 protocol is designed to create.

12 weeks is a starting point, not an endpoint: The clinical study measured outcomes over 12 weeks. Sustainable weight management requires the GR2 principles to become habitual eating patterns rather than a temporary protocol.
